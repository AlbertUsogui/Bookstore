Recommended Books

Usogui

Usogui follows Baku Madarame, a genius gambler who participates in high-stakes. Using manipulation, deception, and sharp insight, he aims to outsmart opponents and seize control of Kagerou, mastering psychological warfare and strategy.

⭐10/10

Liar Game

In Liar Game, honest Nao Kanzaki joins a tournament exploiting trust and greed, risking enormous debts. She partners with genius con artist Akiyama Shinichi, using logic and strategy to outwit ruthless opponents, exploring morality amidst manipulation and betrayal.

⭐9.5/10

One Outs

One Outs features Tokuchi Toua, a cunning pitcher who signs a risky contract based on performance. Mixing psychological warfare and baseball, he uses mental dominance and manipulation to outplay opponents, challenging traditional sports narratives with high-stakes strategy and genius.

⭐9/10

Climber

Climber follows Buntaro Mori, a solitary high school student who discovers his passion for mountaineering. Pushing his physical and mental limits, he ascends perilous peaks, exploring themes of ambition, isolation, and the pursuit of meaning through extreme challenges.

⭐8/10

Akagi

Akagi is a psychological gambling manga that follows Shigeru Akagi, a brilliant mahjong prodigy who rises through dangerous high-stakes games. Known for his audacity and strategic genius, Akagi faces ruthless opponents in a world where survival depends on intellect.

⭐9/10

Ron Kamonohashi

Ron Kamonohashi: Deranged Detective follows the eccentric detective Ron Kamonohashi, who uses unconventional methods to solve bizarre and complex cases. With his troubled past and sharp intellect, Kamonohashi navigates the dark side of crime, blending mystery and psychological depth.

⭐8.5/10

Dr Frost

Dr. Frost is a psychological webtoon about a brilliant yet emotionally detached psychologist, Dr. Frost, who solves complex cases involving human behavior. With his sharp intellect and unconventional methods, he uncovers hidden truths about his clients and the human psyche

⭐9.1/10

Kaiji Ultimate Survivor

Kaiji follows the desperate journey of Kaiji Itou, a down-and-out man drawn into dangerous high-stakes gambling. Facing ruthless opponents, Kaiji uses his wit and nerve to survive deadly games, exploring themes of risk, survival, and human nature.

⭐9.2/10
