Usogui

By: Toshio Sako

⭐5.0

Usogui (The Lie Eater) is a gripping psychological thriller manga by Toshio Sako. It follows Baku Madarame, a genius gambler known as "Usogui," as he navigates a high-stakes world of deceit, strategy, and mind games. With his unmatched intellect and ability to manipulate others, Baku challenges powerful opponents in deadly games organized by the underground organization, Kakerou. The series is renowned for its intricate plots, intense psychological battles, and exploration of human nature, making it a must-read for fans of cerebral storytelling.

Liar Game

By: Shinobu Kaitani

⭐4.8

Liar Game is a psychological thriller manga by Shinobu Kaitani that follows Nao Kanzaki, an honest and naive girl, who gets caught in a high-stakes deception game. Partnering with Akiyama Shinichi, a brilliant con artist, the two face off against cunning opponents in a series of mind-bending psychological battles. The story explores themes of trust, betrayal, and human nature, delivering intense twists and strategic brilliance in every chapter.

Akagi

By: Nobuyuki Fukumoto

⭐4.3

Akagi is a legendary psychological and gambling manga by Nobuyuki Fukumoto. It follows Shigeru Akagi, a fearless and enigmatic prodigy, as he rises through the world of high-stakes mahjong. With his sharp intellect, audacious strategies, and unshakable composure, Akagi faces off against ruthless opponents, often wagering his life. The series is renowned for its intense psychological battles, gripping tension, and exploration of risk, making it a timeless classic for fans of high-stakes storytelling.

⭐4.4

Dr. Frost is a psychological webtoon by Lee Jong-beom that delves into the intricacies of human behavior and emotions. The story follows Dr. Frost, a brilliant yet emotionally detached psychologist, as he solves complex cases involving the human mind. With his keen intellect and unconventional methods, he uncovers hidden truths about his clients and himself. Climber

By: Shin-ichi Sakamoto

⭐4.8

Climber is a gripping manga by Shinichi Sakamoto that follows the intense journey of a young man, Mori Buntarou, who dreams of conquering the world of rock climbing. With determination and passion, Mori faces physical and mental challenges, battling against his own limits and rival climbers. Climber delves into the emotional and psychological struggles of climbing, highlighting themes of perseverance, personal growth, and the pursuit of greatness. Its powerful storytelling and breathtaking artwork make it a must-read for fans of sports and drama.

Monster

By: Naoki Urasawa

⭐4.7

Monster is a psychological thriller manga by Naoki Urasawa that follows Dr. Kenzo Tenma, a Japanese neurosurgeon whose life is turned upside down after saving the life of a young boy who becomes a monstrous killer. As Tenma embarks on a dangerous quest to stop the boy-turned-serial killer, he uncovers a web of conspiracy, morality, and human nature. Monster explores deep themes of good versus evil, guilt, and the consequences of one's actions, with a gripping narrative that keeps readers on the edge of their seats.

Kaiji Ultimate Survivor

By: Nobuyuki Fukumoto

⭐4.2

Kaiji is a thrilling psychological gambling manga by Nobuyuki Fukumoto that follows the story of Kaiji Itou, a down-and-out man who becomes entangled in high-stakes, life-threatening games of chance.
