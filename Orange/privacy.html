Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

• Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, billing/shipping address, and payment details.

• Browsing Information: IP address, device type, browser type, and pages visited.

• Transaction Information: Purchase history and order details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Process and fulfill orders.

• Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

• Improve our website, products, and services.

• Send promotional emails (if you opt-in).

• Ensure a secure shopping experience.

Rules & Regulations

1. Do Not Edit the Website Without Authorization – Only authorized personnel may update or modify website content, settings, or code.

2. Use Strong Passwords – All administrators and users must use strong, unique passwords with a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols.

3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) – Admin accounts must have 2FA enabled to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Do Not Share Login Credentials – Never share login details with others. Each team member should have a unique, restricted account.

5. Regularly Update Software & Plugins – Keep the website’s CMS, themes, plugins, and security patches updated to protect against vulnerabilities.

6. Monitor Website Activity – Regularly check for suspicious activity, unauthorized logins, or unusual file changes.

7. Restrict File Uploads – Limit file uploads to prevent malicious content from being uploaded to the website.

8. Implement HTTPS & SSL Encryption – Ensure all data is encrypted using SSL certificates to protect user information.

9. Backup the Website Regularly – Maintain daily backups to restore the website quickly in case of data loss or cyberattacks.

10. Report Security Issues Immediately – Any suspected security threats or breaches must be reported to the website admin or IT team immediately
